### a

$2\% \times (15ns + \frac{1}{266MHz} \times \frac{32Byte}{128bit} + (1-80\%) \times (60ns + \frac{1}{133MHz} \times \frac{64Byte}{128bit}) \times (1 + 50\%)) \\= 0.02 \times (15 + 3.76 \times 2 + 0.2 \times (60 + 7.52 \times 4) \times 1.5) \\= 0.991ns$

### b
